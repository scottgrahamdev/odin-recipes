Ingredients

  • Heavy Cream
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Butter
  • Fettuccine
  • Garlic Powder
  • Crushed Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Melt butter on low heat and mix in heavy cream.
  2. In another pan, bring water to a boil and cook the fettuccine. Drain when finished.
  3. After butter and heavy cream are combined, add parmesan cheese in small increments until fully melted. Sauce should thicken up.
  4. Add garlic powder and crushed black pepper to taste
  5. Once sauce is completed, add cooked fettuccine and toss thoroughly. Plate and enjoy!
