Ingredients

  • Head of garlic, whole and unpeeled
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Yukon gold potatoes
  • Salt
  • Heavy cream
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Roast the garlic for 30-40 minutes
  3. Boil potatoes for about 15 minutes - should be tender when pierced with fork
  4. Cook butter and cream on low heat until combined
  5. Drain potatoes, mash garlic in with potatoes
  6. Add cream and butter to mashed potatoes and mash until you reach your preferred consistency. Season to taste
  7. Plate and enjoy!
