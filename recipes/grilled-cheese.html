Ingredients

  • Loaf of white bread
  • Cheese of choice
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Spread butter on one side of bread
  2. Place bread in pan on medium heat, butter side down.
  3. Add cheese of your choice and let melt
  4. Place other piece of bread on top of melted cheese, butter side up
  5. Flip and let both sides cook until cheese is fully melted and bread is golden and crispy
  6. Plate and enjoy!
